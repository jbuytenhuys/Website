About Us

We appreciate that your pet is an important member of your family and we are dedicated to offering them the best veterinary care possible. Animal welfare is at the heart of what we do and we strive to give you the best advice to ensure a long, healthy and happy life for your pet.

DeMontfort Vets specialises in the treatment of all domestic pets including; dogs, cats, rabbits, hamsters, hippos, giraffes, lions, gorillas, gazelle, blackwidow spiders and the infamous rattlesnake. Our vets and nurses are highly skilled and are equipped with advanced technology to ensure rapid diagnosis and treatment for your pet.

We are a long established practice with our HQ in DeMonfort, Leicester.

Meet The Team

Jonny Davidson

Jonny graduated from Cambridge University in July 1991. He has worked within the Leicestershire and Northamptonshire area ever since, and has always enjoyed his work at DeMonfort Vets, getting to know the clients and their pets well over the years.

He has a particular interest in orthopaedic surgery, gaining satisfaction from watching animals run happily again after major operations.

David Biles

David started his career in 1990 as a professional basketball player before discovering his love for helping animals in need.

Soon after gaining his phd from DeMontfort University in 2004, he joined the DeMonfort Vets team and has been a valuable asset ever since.

Rick Grimes

Rick qualified from Liverpool University in 2002. After a short stay in Canada, working as a mixed practice vet, he moved to Chippenham for a further 8 years. Rick joined DeMontfort Vets in 2010 to concentrate on small animal medicine.

He likes his case load, loves the clients and enjoys working with a really good team.
